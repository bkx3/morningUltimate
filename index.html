Palo Alto Sunrise Ultimate

Next game on Monday morning, rain or shine

Beginner-friendly, year-round Ultimate on brand new turf.

You can find us at Mayfield Soccer Complex on Page Mill Road and El Camino Real every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 7:00am to 8:00am.

Open in Apple Maps PickupUltimate page

What to expect

A friendly pickup game with mixed experience levels. New players are welcome, and stronger players keep the game fun.

We do not play with subs! If you show up, you'll be able to play for the whole game, even if teams are uneven or if we have more than fourteen players.

What to bring

Cleats, water, a light and dark shirt, and enough time to warm up. The game runs on schedule so people can get to work.
